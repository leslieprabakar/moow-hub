Moow.Hub® — Brand

Brand Guidelines

Standards and best practices for using the Moow.Hub® brand across all channels and materials.

1. Brand Overview

Moow.Hub® is a global wellness ecosystem. Founded in Trivandrum, India, we are dedicated to empowering individuals, organisations, and communities with accessible wellness programmes.

Our brand represents yoga, wellness, preventive healthcare, education, and community impact — building a healthier world, one pose at a time.

Taglines:

  • "Wear a pose. Awaken an ecosystem."
  • "Building a healthier world, one pose at a time."

Website: https://moow-hub.vercel.app

2. Logo Usage

The primary logo is Moow.Hub®, with a terracotta dot separating the two words.

Available Versions

  • Full colour
  • Monochrome (black)
  • Reversed (white on dark backgrounds)

Logo files are available for download from the Partner Portal.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

  • Minimum clear space: Height of the "M" on all sides
  • Minimum size: 24px height (digital), 10mm height (print)

Do NOT

  • Stretch, distort, rotate, or alter the logo
  • Add outlines, shadows, or effects to the logo
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Use the logo as a pattern or decorative element
  • Position the logo in proximity to other logos without prior approval

4. Typography

Playfair Display

Headings — Serif

Weights 400–800, italic variants available

Use for: Hero text, section headers, product names, pricing

Inter

Body Text — Sans-Serif

Weights 300–700

Use for: Body copy, navigation, forms, descriptions, meta text

Both fonts are loaded from Google Fonts.

Font Rules

  • Do not modify fonts (outlined, shadowed, stretched)
  • Do not use decorative fonts in place of brand fonts
  • Font sizes should maintain hierarchy and readability

5. Imagery & Photography

  • Use authentic, diverse, inclusive imagery
  • Show real people practising yoga and wellness activities
  • Warm, natural lighting preferred
  • Avoid overly staged or stock-photo aesthetics
  • Product photography: clean backgrounds, natural styling
  • QR codes should be clearly scannable in all materials
  • Maintain colour consistency with brand palette in photo treatment

6. QR Code System

QR codes are a core brand element linking physical products to digital content.

  • Pre-approved QR code templates must be used
  • QR codes should have adequate quiet zone (white border) for scannability
  • Minimum QR code size: 20mm × 20mm (print), 80px × 80px (digital)
  • Always test QR codes before production
  • QR codes must link to verified Moow.Hub® content only

7. Co-Branding Rules

Co-branding requires prior written approval from Antony.Formaliste@gmail.com.

Submission Requirements

  • Detailed mockup of the proposed co-branded material
  • Intended use and distribution channels
  • Duration of use
  • Territory of distribution

Placement Rules

  • Moow.Hub® logo must be at least equal in size to partner logo
  • Moow.Hub® logo must not be placed below partner logo
  • Maintain clear space around Moow.Hub® logo
  • Both logos must be clearly legible
  • Co-branded materials must comply with all brand guidelines

8. Prohibited Uses

DO NOT:

  • Use Moow.Hub® brand in connection with illegal, harmful, or controversial products or activities
  • Use Moow.Hub® brand in a defamatory, misleading, or reputation-harming manner
  • Register or attempt to register Moow.Hub® trademarks, domain names, or social media handles
  • Create derivative versions of any Moow.Hub® brand assets
  • Use Moow.Hub® brand after termination of Partnership Agreement
  • Alter, modify, or recreate any Moow.Hub® brand assets
  • Use Moow.Hub® brand to imply endorsement without authorization

9. Approval Process

All brand usage outside pre-approved templates requires approval.

  • Submit requests to Antony.Formaliste@gmail.com
  • Include: intended use, mockup, distribution channels, territory, duration
  • Response time: 5 business days
  • No use shall commence until written approval is received
  • Moow.Hub® reserves the right to revoke approval at any time

10. Brand Audit Rights

  • Moow.Hub® reserves the right to conduct periodic brand audits
  • Partners must cooperate and provide access to all branded materials within 5 business days of request
  • Non-compliance may result in termination of brand usage rights

11. Digital Applications

  • Website: Use approved colour palette and typography
  • Social Media: Use approved profile pictures and cover images
  • Email Signatures: Use approved template
  • Mobile App: Follow digital guidelines
  • All digital materials must meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards

12. Print Applications

  • Business cards, letterheads, brochures follow approved templates
  • Use high-resolution logos (minimum 300 DPI)
  • FSC-certified paper preferred for environmental responsibility
  • Eco-friendly inks preferred
  • Use approved Pantone equivalents for spot colour printing

13. Merchandise Applications

  • T-Shirts: Use approved print templates (front, back, sleeve placements)
  • Packaging: Use approved label and tag designs
  • All merchandise must use approved artwork files
  • Material quality must meet brand standards (see product catalogue)
