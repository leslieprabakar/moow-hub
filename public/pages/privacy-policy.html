Moow.Hub® — Legal

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Moow.Hub® collects, uses, protects and shares your personal data.

1. Introduction

Moow.Hub® ("Moow.Hub", "we", "us", "our") is a global wellness ecosystem founded in Pondicherry (Puducherry), India, offering e-commerce for yoga and wellness merchandise, partner programmes, wellness education, a QR-code ecosystem, and a digital content platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share and protect your personal data when you visit our website at https://moow-hub.vercel.app, use our services, or interact with us in any capacity.

By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please do not use our website or provide us with your personal data.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of applicable data protection legislation, the data controller is:

  • Company: Moow.Hub®
  • Email: Antony.Formaliste@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 9342334305
  • Location: Pondicherry (Puducherry), India
  • Website: https://moow-hub.vercel.app

3. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data depending on how you interact with our platform:

3.1 Account Registration

When you create an account, we collect your full name, email address, phone number (optional), and password. Passwords are encrypted via Supabase Auth using bcrypt hashing. We do not store plain-text passwords at any point.

3.2 Profile Data

Once registered, you may provide additional profile information including your name, email, phone number, and default currency preference.

3.3 Checkout and Shipping Data

When you place an order, we collect your full name, delivery address (address lines, city, state, postal code, country), and phone number for shipping and fulfilment purposes.

3.4 Order Data

We maintain order records including order number (format: MOOW-YYYY-######), items purchased, subtotal, shipping charges, tax, total amount, currency, payment method, order status, and tracking information.

3.5 Payment Data

Payments are processed through Razorpay (for INR transactions) or Stripe (for non-INR transactions). We do NOT store any credit card details, debit card details, or banking credentials on our servers. Payment processing is handled entirely by these third-party payment processors in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.

3.6 Cart and Wishlist Data

We store your product selections, quantities, and size preferences in your cart and wishlist to provide a seamless shopping experience.

3.7 Contact Form Data

When you submit a contact or partnership enquiry form, we collect your name, email, phone number, organisation name, location, organisation type, and your message.

3.8 Browsing and Analytics Data

When analytics are enabled, we may collect page views, session data, and device information (browser type, operating system, screen resolution) to understand how our platform is used and to improve user experience.

3.9 Rate Limiting Data

For security purposes, we automatically collect IP addresses, endpoint request details, and attempt counts to prevent abuse and protect our platform from malicious activity.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfil your orders, handle payments, and deliver products to you
  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your user account
  • Transactional Communications: To send order confirmations, password reset emails, email verification links, and partner programme welcome emails
  • Service Improvement: To analyse usage patterns, improve our products and services, and enhance the user experience
  • Customer Support: To respond to your enquiries, resolve issues, and provide assistance
  • Platform Security: To prevent fraud, enforce rate limiting, and protect our platform and users from security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, tax requirements, and legal obligations

5. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent legislation, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to fulfil our contractual obligations to you, including order processing, account management, and service delivery
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests in maintaining platform security, preventing fraud, improving our services, and ensuring business continuity
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent for specific purposes, such as receiving marketing communications or enabling analytics tracking. You may withdraw consent at any time
  • Legal Obligation: Processing is necessary to comply with legal requirements, including maintaining tax records, anti-money laundering regulations, and responding to lawful requests from authorities

6. Data Sharing

We share your personal data only with the following third-party service providers and only to the extent necessary to provide our services:

6.1 Payment Processors

  • Razorpay (India) — For processing INR payments. Razorpay processes payment data in accordance with PCI-DSS standards
  • Stripe (International) — For processing non-INR payments. Stripe processes payment data in accordance with PCI-DSS standards

6.2 Database and Hosting

  • Supabase — Provides PostgreSQL database hosting and authentication services. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Vercel — Provides website hosting and serverless function infrastructure

6.3 Email Services

  • Resend — Used exclusively for sending transactional emails such as order confirmations, password resets, and account verification messages

6.4 Currency Data

  • OpenExchangeRates — Used for real-time currency conversion data to display accurate pricing across supported regions

6.5 Important

We do NOT sell your personal data to any third parties. We may share your data if required by law, court order, or legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

7. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be processed in India, the United States, and other countries where our service providers operate. When we transfer data across borders, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.

Supported Regions: India, United Kingdom, European Union, United States, United Arab Emirates, Singapore, Germany, France.

We ensure that any international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data receives an equivalent level of protection regardless of where it is processed.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after a deletion request
  • Order Data: Retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting requirements
  • Cart and Wishlist Data: Deleted after 90 days of inactivity
  • Rate Limiting Data: Maintained in a 15-minute rolling window
  • Email Logs: Retained for 1 year for service quality and troubleshooting
  • Currency Rate Cache: Refreshed every 1 hour

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR, DPDPA (India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act), CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), and other applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request)
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interests, including for direct marketing purposes
  • Right Not to Be Subject to Automated Decision-Making: You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with a supervisory authority in your jurisdiction

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. If this changes, we will provide a clear opt-out mechanism
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use the following technologies to enhance your experience:

  • localStorage: Used to store authentication tokens and user preferences (such as currency, language, and theme settings) directly in your browser

No third-party tracking cookies are currently active on our website. Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager are currently disabled. If we enable these or any other tracking technologies in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide appropriate cookie consent mechanisms in compliance with applicable regulations.

11. Security

We implement robust technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Password Encryption: All passwords are encrypted via Supabase Auth using bcrypt hashing — plain-text passwords are never stored
  • Authentication: JWT Bearer token authentication for all API requests
  • Input Sanitisation: XSS input sanitisation across all user-submitted data to prevent cross-site scripting attacks
  • Rate Limiting: 5 attempts per 15 minutes per IP address to prevent brute-force attacks and abuse
  • Webhook Security: Razorpay webhook signature verification (HMAC SHA-256) and Stripe webhook verification
  • No Card Storage: We do not store any credit or debit card details on our servers — all payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers
  • HTTPS: All data in transit is encrypted via TLS/HTTPS across our entire platform

12. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under the applicable age, we will take prompt steps to delete that information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at Antony.Formaliste@gmail.com and we will investigate and remove the data.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Communicate material changes via email or a prominent notice on our website
  • Where required by law, obtain your consent before applying significant changes

Your continued use of our website and services after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
